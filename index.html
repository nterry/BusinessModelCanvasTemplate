The Business Model Canvas : "The Drill"

Joshua Rowden

Key Partners

-Similar, yet not-so-similar apps or businesses advertising to maximize advertisement revenue (similar interests means more ad clicks/taps)

-Red Cross, The National Emergency Management Association (NEMA), International Association of Emergency Managers (IAEM), etc, to increase emergency preparedness amongst people in general

Key Activities

-Creating an app with an idea that's never been seen before

-Providing meaningful updates to the app (noticeable difference) that users will enjoy/get more out of

Value Proposition

-An app that nobody's seen until recently. This may cause others to follow in our footsteps.

-Meaningful, fast, noticeable updates to add in all different kinds of emergencies over time

-A competitive casual application without any cheaters or hackers

Customer Relationship

-Meaningful, steady updates

-Provide a free app to users

-Provide a competitive implementation by completing "drills"

Customer Segments

-People who want to be prepared for disasters and/or emergencies in general

-People who want to use the app to compete with other people'

-Businesses could have a customized version or a standard version of the app required by employees to have on while at work (or possibly home)

Key Resources

-Developers
    -Needed to create the app and                to also provide updates

-The Internet
    -Means of advertising and                   delivering app as promised

Channels

-The Internet
    -Most, if not all, advertising will be         done on the internet

    -Means of delivering the app:               Google Play Store or the iOS App        Store

Cost Structure

-Advertisements to maximize awareness of the app, whether it be over the internet, a dinosaur (newspaper),  or something completely different

-Developers to keep updates rolling out

Revenue Streams

-Cost of the app: FREE
    -This means that any money earned will be either
        through donations and/or advertisements.

-Advertisements: - different types:
    -In-app advertisements
    -Notification advertisements
